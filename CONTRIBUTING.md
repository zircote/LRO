# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this research. This document outlines how you can provide feedback, suggest improvements, and collaborate.

## How to Contribute

### Submitting Feedback

Use the GitHub Issue templates to submit structured feedback:

- **[General Feedback](../../issues/new?template=feedback.yml)**. Comments, suggestions, or questions about any section of the paper.
- **[Errata / Corrections](../../issues/new?template=errata.yml)**. Report factual errors, typos, or inaccuracies.
- **[Collaboration Requests](../../issues/new?template=collaboration.yml)**. Propose joint work, extensions, or related research.

### Proposing Corrections or Improvements

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a branch with a descriptive name (e.g., `fix/typo-section-3` or `improve/evaluation-methodology`).
3. Make your changes, keeping commits focused and well-described.
4. Open a Pull Request with a clear explanation of what you changed and why.

For substantive changes (new content, revised arguments, additional references), please open an Issue first to discuss the proposed change before submitting a PR.

### Academic Collaboration

If you are interested in:

- Co-authoring extensions or follow-up work
- Reproducing or extending the evaluation
- Applying the approach in new domains
- Providing formal peer review

Please open a [Collaboration issue](../../issues/new?template=collaboration.yml) or reach out via [GitHub Discussions](../../discussions).

## Discussion Etiquette

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Cite sources when making claims or suggesting alternatives.
- Stay on topic within issue threads.
- Assume good faith from other participants.

Please review the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) before participating.

## What Makes a Good Contribution

- **Specific**: Reference particular sections, paragraphs, or claims.
- **Substantive**: Explain the reasoning behind your suggestion.
- **Sourced**: Include references where applicable.
- **Constructive**: Focus on improving the work, not just critiquing it.
